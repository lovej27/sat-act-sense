Find the right Tutor

We have an eclectic team of instructors from all different academic fields including: high school teachers, college professors, and even MBA graduate students. However, all our tutors have one common purpose: to help each student we tutor get the highest possible score on test day by teaching the most effective test taking and time management strategies.

Guarantee Success

To guarantee the highest level of success, each student receives both a formal assessment (with a complete Diagnostic Test) and an informal meeting to assess the student’s level of competency. Once we have a baseline, we design a personalized program to help maximize the student’s scores on test day.

What we offer

SAT/ACT Sense offers one-on-one or small group tutoring for the PSAT/SAT, ACT, Subject Tests, and academic subjects, in your home, and on your schedule. Tutoring lessons can also be scheduled at local libraries, after school facilities, and community centers.

Small Group Tutoring

From two to five students, who will work with a trained and experienced tutor that will help assess the needs of the group as well as each individual student. Group tutoring is more cost effective when compared to one-on-one.
